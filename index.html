About Sabzi Mandi

A local, family-owned international grocery store serving the Renton community with fresh food and familiar flavors.

Sabzi Mandi International Foods & Groceries is your one-stop market for everyday essentials and authentic ingredients from across South Asia and beyond. Whether you’re shopping for weekly groceries, craving a favorite snack from back home, or discovering new flavors, we’re here to make it easy, friendly, and affordable.
